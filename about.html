Our History

Established on 13th August 2016, Team Mavericks was founded by the Senior students just to help encourage and enhance the students persona so that they can take a foot forward and emphasize on individual and social growth. In addition, then we made it a central committe of college totally dedicated for sdtudent development activities organizing events, awareness programs, and creative workshops. Idea behind setting the team's name Maverick is, we think a Maverick is the one who thinks and acts in a different way. So to plant the seed of this idealogy everywhere we developed Team Mavericks.

Who we are

Introducing Team Mavericks, a dynamic and passionate group of individuals hailing from KIT's College of Engineering in the vibrant city of Kolhapur. At Team Mavericks, we firmly believe that learning should be an enjoyable and enriching experience, which is why we have made it our Motto 'learning with fun'.

Throughout the academic year, our team of Mavericks diligently organizes and conducts a diverse array of sessions, encompassing both technical and non-technical subjects. We understand the significance of honing skills and expanding knowledge in today's fast-paced world. That's why we go above and beyond to provide our team members with a platform to learn and grow, fostering an environment where curiosity is nurtured and potential is unleashed.

Our technical sessions cover a wide range of cutting-edge topics, such as programming languages, software development, data analysis, artificial intelligence, and more. Through hands-on workshops, interactive discussions, and informative presentations, we empower our members with the latest tools and techniques in their respective fields. But it doesn't stop there. We believe that personal growth and development go hand in hand with technical expertise. That's why we also dedicate ourselves to organizing non-technical sessions that cover subjects like communication skills, leadership development, teamwork, creativity, and stress management. These sessions equip our team members with essential soft skills needed to thrive in today's interconnected world.

Our Vision

Just like The Mavericks means unorthodox and independent, we are a vibrant and dynamic group of passionate students dedicated to fostering a spirit of innovation, creativity, and collaboration within our institute. With the agenda of 'Learning with Fun', our primary aim is to create a platform where students can explore their interests, expand their horizons, and enhance their skills through a diverse range of technical and non-technical events.

With a focus on both educational and recreational activities, we curate an exciting lineup of events throughout the academic year, catering to the diverse interests and talents of our fellow students. Whether you are a budding engineer, an aspiring entrepreneur, a talented artist, or simply someone with a hunger for knowledge, we have something for everyone.
