BODHANTRA

BODHANTRA, organized by Team Mavericks, is a five-day college event for first-year students, offering a comprehensive blend of technical and non-technical sessions. Through interactive sessions, thought-provoking discussions, and team-building challenges, BODHANTRA fosters a culture of curiosity, continuous learning, and teamwork. Creative competitions encourage participants to showcase their talents and innovative thinking. With a focus on "Learning With Fun," BODHANTRA aims to create a joyful and enriching learning experience, empowering first-year students with valuable life skills for personal and professional growth.

INVICTA

INVICTA is a thoughtfully designed workshop series by Team Mavericks, offering a wide range of technical and non-technical workshops for participants of all ages and educational backgrounds. The series aims to promote skill development, stay updated with the latest trends, and provide hands-on experience in various subjects such as web development, ethical hacking, soft skills, mental health, and more. With a focus on inclusivity and accessibility, INVICTA fosters a spirit of lifelong learning and personal growth. Members from Team Mavericks ensure a high-quality learning experience, empowering attendees to cultivate a well-rounded skill set for their personal and professional advancement.

CARNIVAL

CARNIVAL, organized by Team Mavericks, is an engaging and vibrant event inspired by IIT Techfest. It offers a delightful mix of entertainment and fun activities, including carefully curated games that encourage student participation, foster brotherhood, and develop cognitive and physical skills. The event also features interactive exhibits that provide hands-on learning experiences and mind-boggling competitions spanning various disciplines to showcase participants' talents and problem-solving abilities. CARNIVAL aims to inspire creativity, exploration of new interests, and lasting connections within the college community while providing cherished memories of an inclusive and enjoyable celebration.

ARCANE

ARCANE, organized by Team Mavericks, is an exhilarating virtual treasure hunt that fosters an interactive and educational experience for participants of all ages and skill levels. Structured into multiple rounds with varying levels of difficulty, ARCANE challenges participants to gather information and solve questions. This engaging event promotes critical thinking, research skills, and creativity while encouraging teamwork and knowledge-sharing among participants from different locations. As a fun and intellectually stimulating activity, ARCANE reflects Team Mavericks' dedication to creating inclusive and enriching experiences for students.

SCHOOL VISIT

Team Mavericks actively conducts school visits in rural areas of Kolhapur, aiming to bridge the technology gap between urban and rural regions. These visits involve practical demonstrations, workshops, and presentations to educate students about emerging topics like AI, robotics, blockchain, and virtual reality. The team provides career guidance and fosters interactive conversations to broaden students' understanding of potential career opportunities. This initiative reflects the team's commitment to social responsibility, benefiting both students and team members through knowledge expansion and personal growth.
